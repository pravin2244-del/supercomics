Marvel Comics

Marvel Comics is an American comic book publisher. Known for creating iconic characters and complex, interconnected storylines, Marvel has built a vast and beloved universe.

Thor

God of Thunder and protector of the Nine Realms.

DC Comics

DC Comics is one of the largest and oldest American comic book companies. It is home to some of the world's most famous superheroes and supervillains, as well as a sprawling multiverse.

Popular DC Characters

Superman

The Man of Steel, a symbol of hope and truth.

Batman

The Dark Knight, protector of Gotham City.

Wonder Woman

Amazonian warrior princess and ambassador for peace.

Aquaman

King of Atlantis and ruler of the seven seas.

Flash

The fastest man alive with lightning speed.

About Marvel & DC

Marvel and DC are the two biggest comic book publishers in the world, often referred to as "The Big Two." They have both been creating comic books for decades, shaping popular culture with their extensive roster of characters and compelling narratives.

Marvel History

Founded in 1939 as Timely Comics, Marvel Comics began its golden age with characters like the Human Torch and Captain America. The silver age in the 1960s saw the creation of many of their most famous heroes by Stan Lee, Jack Kirby, and Steve Ditko.
